### Syntax

    "priority": <integer>
    - Presets with higher priority override presets with lower priority.
    - Effects stacks if their category allows stacking (e.g. "rim_lighting" effect can't stack).

    "panel": <ID> (optional)
    "frame": <ID> (optional)
    "icon": <ID> (optional)
    "effects": [<IDs>] (optional)
    "filter": {<keys>} (see parent .readme.txt for details)
